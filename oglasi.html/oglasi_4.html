The door opens, revealing a spacious hall that instantly envelops you in a warm atmosphere. On one side, a vast living room bathed in light is offered to you. With its high ceilings, its original parquet flooring, its elegant moldings and exposed beams, this space exudes conviviality and relaxation. On the other side, the dining room, full of authenticity, naturally extends towards a fully equipped and functional kitchen, perfect for large meals, with direct access to the Granite terrace. The latter, bordered by an openwork guardrail of hollow tiles, evokes the typical architectural charm of Clisson. This place is ideal for enjoying a morning coffee, bathed in the first rays of the sun. The ground floor is completed by a back kitchen, a practical laundry room, a separate toilet, and a storage room with independent access and stairs leading upstairs, offering all the comfort necessary for everyday life. Upstairs, a period staircase, with an impressive ceiling height, leads to the sleeping area. On one side, a bathroom designed for comfort, with bathtub and shower, as well as a spacious master bedroom with a dressing room. On the other side of the landing, a long corridor leads to three bedrooms with original parquet flooring, one of which has a private shower room and a marble fireplace. At the end of the corridor, adjoining the staircase leading to the ground floor storage room, a room, currently used for storage, could easily be transformed into a fifth bedroom, a shower room or an office. The vast garden with trees, a true haven of peace, quickly makes you forget the hustle and bustle of the city and hides a surprise: a delightful stone outbuilding, decorated with its Genoese and windows framed in bricks. Full of character, this outbuilding houses a room with a beaten earth floor and an attic, offering additional space with a thousand possibilities. This garden has two parking spaces under a carport, and can accommodate several additional vehicles. In addition, a cellar, ideal for wine lovers and a boiler room (city gas boiler, cast iron radiators). Possible guest room project. This characterful residence, a true witness to a bygone era, lends itself to a peaceful life in the heart of Clisson, where each stone seems to whisper stories of yesteryear, in a setting where everything is within reach.
