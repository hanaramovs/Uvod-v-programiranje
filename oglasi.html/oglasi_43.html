In the heart of the Baie de Somme, this property offers 165 sqm of living space on approx. 960 sqm of land. The town centre, port and sea wall are all within walking distance. With a charming little house in very good condition and a garage which can also be converted. Ground floor: entrance hall, 55 sqm living room with fireplace, two offices, fitted kitchen, utility room/ toilet. Upstairs there are four bedrooms, one of which has a balcony, and a bathroom. The attic can be converted. Large cellar.
