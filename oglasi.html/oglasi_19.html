It includes an entrance, a living room with insert, a dining room, an equipped kitchen with veranda, an office, a bedroom with dressing room and shower room, a laundry room. Upstairs: a large landing room serving 2 bedrooms with parquet floors, a bathroom with shower. In annex: a tiled garage with storage rooms (old boxes) and extra bedroom. Excellent state of maintenance with quality old materials. Possibility of sale fully furnished. EXCLUSIVE of which 3.54 % fees incl. VAT at the buyer's expense.
