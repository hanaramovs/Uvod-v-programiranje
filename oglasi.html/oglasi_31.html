This house built in 2013, develops 158m2 and offers an intimate living room as well as a bright and welcoming living room, a fully equipped dining kitchen for your comfort, 4 spacious bedrooms including a master suite, and finally a workshop/laundry room. Outside, in the heart of a very well-kept garden, you will discover a pretty salt-treated swimming pool and a pleasant travertine terrace accompanied by a double pergola. To complete everything, a pétanque court is also present for moments of relaxation with friends or family. Equipment: Air conditioning, electric gate, videophone, garden shed, double glazing, electric roller shutters, optical fiber, insert fireplace, mosaic swimming pool. Enjoy the best of both worlds, combining the tranquility of a charming village with proximity to the vibrant town of Uzès. For more information or to arrange a visit, do not hesitate to contact me.
