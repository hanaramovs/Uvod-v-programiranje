A century-old plane tree will wonderfully play the role of a large parasol for the summer season, recalling the tradition of planting them in front of Mas to enjoy natural shade. INTERIORS OF THE MAS: This Provencal Mas has retained the authenticity and charm of yesteryear, with its volumes, its French ceilings, its walls, and exposed stone vaults. The roof is new with trillatte insulation and the main masonry work is completed. The property offers: A dwelling of approximately 220sqm on 2 levels, with on the ground floor the living spaces, a bedroom, a laundry room-back kitchen, and a garage. Upstairs, 3 suites are planned with the possibility of arranging 4 bedrooms in these volumes if desired.
